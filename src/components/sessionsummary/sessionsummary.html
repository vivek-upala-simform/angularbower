FreeWire’s Boost Charger is a powerful battery-integrated electric vehicle charger. Easy to connect with existing infrastructure, it can be set up without costly construction or permitting. With 160 kWh of battery capacity and 120 kW output, Boost Charger is ready for current and next generation EVs. Boost Charger enables ultrafast charging using the same infrastructure as L2 chargers at up to a 40% lower cost of installation versus other fast chargers. Deliver high-quality power while significantly reducing demand charges with Boost Charger’s battery-integrated platform.

Lower Energy Costs

Unparalleled Speed

Deliver up to 480 miles of range with a single, one-hour charge thanks to 160 kWh of lithium-ion energy storage and power-boosting, high-density silicon carbide technology.

Intelligent Charging

Get full visibility into charger status and usage with Freewire's Activity Management Platform(AMP). AMP lets you regulate power delivery between utilities, admins and end-customers.

Easier Setup

Avoid panel upgrades, new transformers, costly infrastructure updates and permitting delays. Boost charger can be powered from a single-phase connection and installed in places that couldn't previously support higher energy demand.
